# 📊 SCT_DA_3 – HR Analytics Dashboard (Excel)

![HR Dashboard](HR%20Dashboard.jpeg)

## 📌 Project Overview

The **HR Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard designed to analyze employee attrition, workforce demographics, salary distribution, and retention trends.

The dashboard helps HR professionals identify key factors influencing employee turnover and supports data-driven workforce planning through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze employee attrition across departments.
- Monitor overall workforce statistics.
- Evaluate employee retention by age group.
- Compare salary across different education fields.
- Understand gender distribution within the organization.
- Identify income bands with higher attrition rates.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains HR employee records including:

- Employee Count
- Department
- Job Role
- Gender
- Age Group
- Salary
- Education Field
- Attrition Status
- Income Band

## 📌 Key Insights

- Employees with lower income bands show higher attrition rates.
- Sales department has the highest employee attrition.
- Most employees belong to the 26–35 age group.
- Marketing graduates have the highest average salary.
- Male employees represent the majority of the workforce.
